Ferrothorn

Hazards support

One of the best stealth rock and spikes placer in the game. Great utility with knock off.

Item: Leftovers

Ability: Iron Barbs

Nature: Careful

Moveset

  • Stealth Rock
  • Spikes
  • Knock Off
  • Power Whip

Tentacruel

Rapid spin and poison support

Stealth Rock removal helps Thundurus-Therian dominate late game. Toxic spikes and protect stall
can also be useful. Scald for decent STAB, especially in rain + burn possibility.

Item: Black Sludge

Ability: Rain Dish

Nature: Timid

Moveset

  • Scald
  • Toxic Spikes
  • Rapid Spin
  • Protect

Garchomp

Attack Sweeper

Along with Thundurus-Therian, Garchomp is another great sweeping threat
that any opposing team must take into account.

Item: Yache Berry

Ability: Rough Skin

Nature: Jolly

Moveset

  • Stone Edge
  • Outrage
  • Earthquake
  • Swords Dance

Blissey

Cure and SpA wall

Blissey heals your team from status and can wall multiple SpA sweepers.
Wish can also be substituted for Soft-Boiled for even more support capability.

Item: Leftovers

Ability: Natural Cure

Nature: Bold

Moveset

  • Soft-Boiled
  • Seismic Toss
  • Toxic
  • Aromatherapy
Back to the top of the page

Thundurus-Therian

SpA Sweeper

Although vulnerable to stealth rocks, Thundurus-Therians immense offensive potential makes him
well worth it with proper support. Can exchange leftovers for life orb, especially if Blissey has wish.

Item: Leftovers

Ability: Volt Absorb

Nature: Timid

Moveset

  • Substitute
  • Thunderbolt
  • Hidden Power Ice
  • Nasty Plot
Back to the top of the page

Heatran

SpD defensive support

Taunt, roar and protect gives Heatran great supporting capabilities along with Lava Plume for an effective STAB.

Item: Leftovers

Ability: Flash Fire

Nature: Calm

Moveset

  • Taunt
  • Roar
  • Lava Plume
  • Protect
