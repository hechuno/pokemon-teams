Rapid spin and poison support

can also be useful. Scald for decent STAB + burn possibility.

Item: Leftovers Ability: Torrent Nature: Bold

Moveset

  • Scald
  • Toxic
  • Rapid Spin
  • Protect

Bronzong

Stealth rocks setter

Stealth Rocks, toxic and protect make Bronzong a great support pokemon.

Item: Leftovers Ability: Iron Barbs Nature: Careful

Moveset

  • Psychic
  • Toxic
  • Stealth Rock
  • Protect
